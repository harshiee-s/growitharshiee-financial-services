GSTR-2B is a static auto-drafted ITC statement generated by the GST portal for each taxpayer. Before claiming Input Tax Credit in your GSTR-3B return, it's mandatory to reconcile your Purchase Register with GSTR-2B to ensure every ITC claim is backed by a supplier-filed invoice.

Use this tool to instantly match your records, identify mismatches, spot missing invoices, and know exactly how much CGST, SGST, and IGST credit is available to you this period.

🔍 How the Reconciliation Works

The tool matches invoices using a composite key of GSTIN + Invoice Number — the standard GST matching criterion. Here's what each status means:

StatusMeaningITC Treatment
✔ MatchedInvoice found in both PR and 2B with matching amounts (within ₹1 tolerance)Fully available — claim in GSTR-3B
⚠ MismatchInvoice found in both, but amounts differ in one or more fieldsAvailable per 2B value; reconcile with supplier
PR OnlyInvoice in your books but not yet in GSTR-2B portalCannot claim yet — supplier may not have filed
2B OnlyInvoice visible in 2B portal but missing from your purchase registerVerify and add to PR before claiming

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Both CSV (.csv) and Excel (.xlsx/.xls) formats are supported. Ensure the first row contains headers. The tool auto-detects common column names like "Inv No.", "Invoice Number", "GSTIN No.", "Taxable Amount", "Taxable Amt", etc.
ITC is calculated from all Matched and Mismatch records using the GSTR-2B portal values, since the portal data is authoritative for ITC purposes. PR-Only records are excluded as the supplier hasn't reflected those invoices yet.
Contact your supplier to issue a credit/debit note correcting the discrepancy, or amend your purchase register if the entry was incorrectly recorded on your end. Claim ITC only on the amount reflected in 2B.
Under Section 16(2)(aa) of the CGST Act (effective from FY 2022–23), ITC is available only on invoices reflected in GSTR-2B. PR-Only invoices should not be claimed until the supplier files their GSTR-1/IFF and the invoice appears in your 2B.
