📋 Budget 2025: New Tax Slabs

The government has revised the tax slabs for the New Tax Regime in Budget 2025 to provide more relief to the middle class. Here are the updated rates:

Income Slab Tax Rate
Up to ₹4,00,000 Nil
₹4,00,001 to ₹8,00,000 5%
₹8,00,001 to ₹12,00,000 10%
₹12,00,001 to ₹16,00,000 15%
₹16,00,001 to ₹20,00,000 20%
₹20,00,001 to ₹24,00,000 25%
Above ₹24,00,000 30%
Rebate Update Full tax rebate under section 87A is available for income up to ₹12 Lakhs under the new regime.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Under the New Regime (2025), most deductions are removed (like 80C, HRA, LTA). However, you can still claim the Standard Deduction of ₹75,000 and the employer's contribution to NPS under section 80CCD(2).
Salaried individuals can choose between the old and new regime every year. However, individuals with business or professional income (freelancers, consultants) can opt back to the old regime only once in a lifetime.
A Health and Education Cess of 4% is added to the computed income tax amount in both regimes. Our calculator automatically applies this 4% to give you the final payable amount.
