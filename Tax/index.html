📋 Budget 2025: New Tax Slabs

The government revised the slabs for the New Tax Regime in Budget 2025. These rates apply on taxable income (after standard deduction).

Taxable Income SlabTax Rate
Up to ₹4,00,000Nil
₹4,00,001 – ₹8,00,0005%

Key Points Full 87A rebate (up to ₹60,000) available if taxable income ≤ ₹12 lakh. Salaried individuals with gross salary up to ₹12.75L pay zero tax (₹12L + ₹75K std deduction). Cess of 4% is added on top of tax in all cases.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Under the New Regime (2025), most deductions are removed (80C, HRA, LTA etc.). You can still claim Standard Deduction of ₹75,000 and employer NPS contribution under 80CCD(2).
Salaried individuals can switch every year. Those with business/professional income can opt back to old regime only once in a lifetime.
4% cess is added to the income tax computed under both regimes. The income tax portal shows tax and cess as separate line items — this calculator now shows the same breakdown so you can reconcile easily.
The IT portal asks for taxable income (after std deduction). This calculator accepts gross salary and subtracts std deduction for you. If you enter the same taxable income in both, the results will match. Use the toggle above to switch input mode.
