💡 How to Use This Tool

This tool safely runs entirely in your browser. No financial data is ever uploaded to our servers.

  • Download your Bank Statement Export your statement from your banking portal in `.csv` format. Ensure it has a header row.
  • Map Your Columns Open the CSV to check which column number corresponds to Date, Reference, Notes, and Amount. Enter those numbers below.
  • Set Account Ledgers Define your primary Bank Account name and the default Counter Account (e.g., Suspense A/c or Salary A/c).
  • Process & Download Click process to generate double-entry ledgers dynamically based on deposits and withdrawals, then export as a clean CSV.
Privacy First All processing happens via JavaScript right here on your device. Safe for client data and strict NDA compliance.
