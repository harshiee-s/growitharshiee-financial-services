Real Results

Case Study: Fixing the ITC Gap

How proper bookkeeping saves real money.

1. The Problem

A local agency had 6 months of messy books. Their GSTR-2B data didn't match their vendor invoices, risking a major loss in Input Tax Credit (ITC).

2. The Solution

I migrated their raw data into Zoho Books, reconciled every missing vendor invoice, and established a clean, monthly checklist for their team.

3. The Result

We successfully claimed the missing ITC before the deadline, preventing financial loss, and they are now 100% audit-ready every single month.
