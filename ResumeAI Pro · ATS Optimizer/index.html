🛠️ What ResumeAI Pro Does

Every feature is built around one goal — get your resume past the ATS filter and into a recruiter's hands.

5-Dimension ATS Score

Overall score plus keyword match, formatting, skills and experience — each on a 0–100 scale with animated dials.

Auto Industry Detection

No JD? No problem. AI identifies your industry from the resume itself and applies the most relevant ATS keywords for that sector.

≥85 Score Guaranteed

The rewritten resume is engineered to score 85+ by addressing every structural, keyword, and formatting gap in one pass.

Bullets & Spacing Preserved

PDFs are parsed by Y/X coordinates so indentation and bullets survive. DOCX files are parsed as HTML so every list item is kept.

6 Recruiter-Approved Templates

Modern, Minimal, Executive, Fresher, Tech and Finance — switch live without losing your edits.

DOCX & PDF Export

Generated resumes preserve typography and spacing. Download as Word for editing or PDF for sending.
