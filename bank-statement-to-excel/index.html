✨ Capabilities

Engineered for serious financial work — every feature designed for real CA and bookkeeping workflows.

AI Narration Cleaner

Strips ref numbers, UTRs and clutter. Normalizes shouting-case vendor names into readable descriptions.

Auto-Categorization

Salary, UPI, GST, TDS, Vendor Payment, EMI, Card Spend, Cash Withdrawal — 12 categories built-in.

International Transactions

USD, EUR, GBP, AED, SGD and 30+ currencies parsed correctly — INR equivalent in the column, foreign value preserved.

Duplicate Detection

Catches duplicate entries across merged statements using a date+amount+narration fingerprint.

Private by Design
